**General of The Salvation Army**

General Lyndon Buckingham serves as the international leader of The Salvation Army. A native of New Zealand, he and his wife Commissioner Bronwyn Buckingham have served the Army across multiple territories. His preaching is marked by warmth, humor, and a passionate call for Salvationists to be "influencers for the Kingdom."
