**Author:** William Booth | **Published:** 1872

One of William Booth's earliest published works, this collection documents the methods and achievements of the Christian Mission (which would later become The Salvation Army) in reaching the poor and unchurched of East London. It includes reports on children's work, mothers' meetings, and accounts from individual mission stations.
