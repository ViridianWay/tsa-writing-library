**Author:** William Booth | **Published:** 1907

This collection gathers 35 of William Booth's messages addressed directly to the rank-and-file soldiers of The Salvation Army. Covering topics from faith and repentance to duty and the role of women, these messages offer practical spiritual guidance from the Founder himself.
