**Author:** William Booth | **Published:** 1909

In this practical four-part series, Booth draws on decades of evangelistic experience to teach the art of personal soul-winning. From basic techniques to handling objections, these articles were written for every Salvationist who wanted to become more effective in bringing people to faith.
