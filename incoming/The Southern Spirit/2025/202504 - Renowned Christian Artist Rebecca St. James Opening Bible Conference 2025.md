# 202504 - Renowned Christian Artist Rebecca St. James Opening Bible Conference 2025

* **By:** Not specified
* **Date:** April 01, 2025
* **Location:** Lake Junaluska, NC

The GRAMMY-winning singer/songwriter Rebecca St. James is set to open this year’s Bible Conference with a concert on Sunday, August 3, at 7:00 pm in Stuart Auditorium at Lake Junaluska. The Australian native has been bringing words of life and hope to homes and radios since 1994, earning multiple Dove Awards, nine #1 Christian radio singles, and two GOLD-certified recordings.

You won’t want to miss this incredible night of music and worship or the profound biblical teachings throughout the week from international Salvation Army leaders General Lyndon and Commissioner Bronwyn Buckingham. Register for Bible Conference today at [southernbibleconference.org](https://southernbibleconference.org).
